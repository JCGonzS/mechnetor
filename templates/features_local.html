Features

Single-protein input method:

A graph will be created with your gene and a number of known interactors. This method is useful when you don't know the interactors of your gene of interest, or you want to explore putable effects of mutations on any interaction.
It is triggered by entering a single gene name/identifier in the input box. Select the maximum number of interactors that will be displayed with the "Limit the number of proteins" slider.
Interactors for your gene are determined through BioGRID and sorted according to the evidence (# of publications where they've been reported).
p-value refers to the probability of finding a protein pair that contain the element pair association score is the log-oddsrelation between the number of known interacting protein pairs and the expected number just by chance. we only report values >= 2. The higher, the better.

Data

Protein info

  • UniProt
    • accession, description, gene name, length
  • Pfam
    • protein family domains
  • ELM
    • eukaryotic linear motifs
  • PhosphoSitePlus
    • phosphorylation and acetylation sites
  • COSMIC (v87; released Nov 2018)
    • human cancer mutations

    Protein Interactions

  • BioGRID (version 3.5.165; September 23, 2018)
    • protein-protein interactions
  • 3did (released April 2018)
    • domain-domain interactions
  • ELM
    • linear motif-domain interactions
  • InterPreTS
    • 3D-structure templates interactions
